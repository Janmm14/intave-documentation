  * `intave.trust.green`
  * `intave.trust.yellow`

* Command permissions:
  * `intave.command`

  * Internals:

    * `intave.command.internals.collectivekick`
    * `intave.command.internals.delay`
    * `intave.command.internals.rejoinblock`
    * `intave.command.internals.sendnotify`
